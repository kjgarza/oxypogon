## Workout Components

### 1. Row/Ski Erg (3:00)

- **Technique Steps:**
    - For the rower: Sit with your feet secured, grab the handle with both hands, and start with your knees slightly bent.
    - Push through your heels, extend your legs, lean back slightly, and pull the handle towards your chest, keeping your elbows close.
    - Reverse the motion: Extend your arms, lean forward, and bend your knees to return to the starting position.
    - For the Ski Erg: Stand tall, grip the handles, and initiate the movement by pulling down with your arms while engaging your core and legs.
  
- **Form Tips:**
    - Keep your back straight and core engaged throughout the movement.
    - Use your legs to drive the motion, not just your arms.
  
- **Scaling/Modifications:**
    - If you’re new to rowing or skiing, reduce the time to 2:00 or focus on maintaining a steady pace rather than intensity.
  
---

### 2. Box Step-ups (20 reps)

- **Technique Steps:**
    - Stand in front of the box with your feet hip-width apart.
    - Step up with one foot, pressing through your heel to lift your body onto the box.
    - Bring the other foot up to meet the first, then step back down one foot at a time.
  
- **Form Tips:**
    - Keep your chest up and shoulders back as you step up.
    - Ensure your knee doesn’t go past your toes when stepping up.
  
- **Scaling/Modifications:**
    - Use a lower box if you’re not comfortable with the height.
    - You can also perform step-ups without weights to focus on balance and form.
  
---

### 3. Kettlebell Sumo Deadlift High Pulls (15 reps)

- **Technique Steps:**
    - Stand with your feet wider than shoulder-width apart, toes slightly pointed out, with the kettlebell on the floor between your feet.
    - Hinge at your hips and bend your knees to grab the kettlebell with both hands.
    - Drive through your heels to stand up, pulling the kettlebell up along your body, leading with your elbows.
    - As the kettlebell reaches chest height, lower it back down to the starting position.
  
- **Form Tips:**
    - Keep your back straight and core engaged throughout the lift.
    - Focus on using your legs to initiate the movement, not just your arms.
  
- **Scaling/Modifications:**
    - Use a lighter kettlebell or perform the movement without weight to practice the form.
  
---

### 4. Ring Rows (10 reps)

- **Technique Steps:**
    - Set the rings at a height that allows you to pull your body up comfortably.
    - Grab the rings and lean back, keeping your body straight and feet on the ground.
    - Pull your chest towards the rings, squeezing your shoulder blades together at the top.
    - Lower yourself back down with control.
  
- **Form Tips:**
    - Keep your body in a straight line from head to heels.
    - Avoid letting your hips sag or your shoulders round forward.
  
- **Scaling/Modifications:**
    - If rings are too challenging, you can perform inverted rows on a barbell or use a TRX.
